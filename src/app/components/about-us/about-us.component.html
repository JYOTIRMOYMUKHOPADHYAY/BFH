About Us

Bogmalo foods and hospitality had its inception in the year 2017 with two brands and two outlets in Goa and Bangalore. In just 4 years it has expanded into 6 brands and 13 outlets across Bangalore, Goa, Delhi, Pondicherry, Kolkata, Lucknow and Indore with many more coming up the next few months.

Bogmalo foods and hospitality is a company backed by a very strong team of engineer turned food entrepreneurs who thrive for excellence, quality and innovation!
